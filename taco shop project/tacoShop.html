Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in US?

Early introduction: Tacos were first brought to the U.S. by Mexican workers who migrated to the Southwest for jobs in mines and on railroads. First mention: The earliest known mention of tacos in a U.S. newspaper was in 1905.
